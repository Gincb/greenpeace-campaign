What is Greenpeace?

I.


Greenpeace exists because this fragile earth deserves a voice. It needs solutions. It needs change. It needs action.

II.


They challenge the power of fossil fuel corporations, bolster support for renewable and citizen-powered energy and seek to hold big polluters to account.

III.


Focusing on great global forests and oceans they aim to preserve, protect and restore the most valuable ecosystems for the climate and for biodiversity.

IV.


They want to tear apart the way corporations and governments collude to make a profit at the expense of both people and planet.

V.


For nearly 50 years, Greenpeace has been sailing the world’s oceans protecting our planet and fighting for environmental justice.
